The Perfect PB & J

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is quick and easy to make! Perfect for on the go and perfect for a quick meal at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread (or any bread of your liking) 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter Knife.
  • A cutting board.

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on the top.
  5. With the sharp knife, cafefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Lastly, enjoy!
